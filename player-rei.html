Rei Hino

Sailor Mars • Guardian of Fire & Passion

Power: 90
Speed: 75
Magic: 85

About Rei

Rei Hino is a shrine maiden with powerful spiritual intuition and a fierce sense of duty. Her fiery determination and disciplined nature make her one of the most formidable Sailor Guardians.

Abilities

Main Attack: Fire Soul

Special Ability: Psychic Flame Readings

Personality

Passionate, intuitive, disciplined, and protective. Rei’s fire is both literal and emotional — she burns brightest when defending those she loves.
